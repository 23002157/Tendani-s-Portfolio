2. TicTacToe Dupe

I created a duplicate version of the famous TicTacToe. A two-player game played on a 3x3 grid where players take turns marking spaces with "X" or "O". The first player to get three of their marks in a row either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, wins.

Tools used: Java
